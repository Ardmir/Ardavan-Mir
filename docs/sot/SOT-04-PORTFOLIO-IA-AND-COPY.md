## Approach section

Use four principles:

1. Translate complexity into clarity.
2. Make intelligence explainable.
3. Design for trust before automation.
4. Create systems that scale across teams.

## Copy rules

- Keep copy confident but not inflated.
- Avoid unverified metrics.
- Avoid confidential product details.
- Avoid buzzword overload.
- Make IES and QuickBooks the main story.
- Treat Iranians Who Design as differentiation, not the core hiring claim.
